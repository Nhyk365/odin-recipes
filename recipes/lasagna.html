Lasagna alla Bolognese

What's a Lasagna:

Lasagna alla Bolognese is an institution, the baked lasagna par excellence, the typical Sunday dish. This rich and tasty dish originates from Emilia and, specifically, from the city of Bologna. Lasagna al forno (baked lasagna), however, is known, appreciated, made and remade for the holidays and beyond, tasted and shared all over Italy and abroad as Italy's signature dish.

Lasagna al forno (baked lasagna) is made of fresh egg pasta, served with the classic traditional ragout, béchamel sauce and grated cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Bechamel

Steps:

  1. Prepare your ragout by cooking meat inside your tomato sauce, together with onions, garlic and spices.
  2. In a rectangular casserole cover the bottom with béchamel.
  3. Add lasagna noodles to cover it all.
  4. Add your ragout all over the lasagna noodles and top it off with parmesan cheese.
  5. Repeat steps from 2 to 4 as many times as you like, more thick layers will make your lasagna softer and creamier, less layers will make it more crunchy.
  6. Cook in the oven until the top layer is gold and crispy.
